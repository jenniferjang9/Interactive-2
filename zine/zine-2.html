bluetooth smart speaker

Smart speakers are devices gives us a hands-free interface that can perform tasks that used to require a screen or additional input devices such as a mouse or keyboard. They have gained popularity due to the many conveniences and benefits they bring to make day to day tasks a little easier.

But did you also know...

These devices are capable of being a microphone that records your conversations making it accessible to big corporate companies that uses and stores this data in means to profit from it. Essentially you are running the risk of your own security by allowing these devices to live in your very home.
